================================================================================
                     INTERVIEW INSIGHTS - HOW TO USE THE PROGRAM
================================================================================

WHAT IT DOES
------------
Enter a candidate's scores and feedback. The program returns a risk level,
a list of specific issues, and coaching recommendations tailored to the
client type. Use it before an interview to predict risk, or after to
understand why a candidate failed.


================================================================================
                              THE FORM — QUICK GUIDE
================================================================================

1. INTERVIEW MODE
   Pre-Interview  — predict risk before the interview happens
   Post-Interview — explain why the candidate failed after the fact

2. CLIENT TYPE
   Corporate   — weights communication and executive presence higher
   Startup     — weights confidence and cultural fit higher
   Consulting  — weights technical and communication equally high

   This changes how scores are calculated. Set it to match the actual client.

3. CANDIDATE DETAILS
   Name, role, years of experience, prior client rejections.
   Each prior rejection deducts 5 points from the final score (max -20).

4. SCORES (0 to 10)
   Internal Score      — your agency's overall rating
   Technical Score     — role-specific technical ability
   Communication Score — clarity and articulation
   Confidence Score    — presence and self-assurance
   Cultural Fit Score  — alignment with the client's culture

   Slider colors:
     Green  = 8.0 and above (strong)
     Yellow = 6.0 to 7.9   (moderate)
     Red    = below 6.0    (weak)

5. QUALITATIVE FEEDBACK
   Recruiter Notes  — your observations before the interview
   Client Feedback  — what the client said after (optional in Pre mode)

   Be specific. The program scans this text for keywords like:
     "nervous", "hesitant", "rambling", "unprepared", "gaps in knowledge"
   The more detail you write, the more issues it can detect.

6. SUBMIT
   "Predict Risk"    — Pre-Interview mode
   "Analyse Failure" — Post-Interview mode


================================================================================
                              READING THE RESULTS
================================================================================

RISK HEADER
  Shows the mode, risk level badge, summary sentence, and score out of 100.
  Risk levels:
    LOW      75-100  Good to go
    MEDIUM   55-74   Needs coaching
    HIGH     35-54   Serious prep needed
    CRITICAL 0-34    Do not submit yet

SCORE BREAKDOWN
  Bar chart of all 5 dimensions. Footer shows weighted total, rejection
  penalty (if any), and final score.

IDENTIFIED ISSUES
  Specific problems detected from low scores and keyword scanning.

RECOMMENDATIONS
  Numbered coaching actions tailored to the issues and client type.
  A confidence issue at a Corporate client gets different advice than
  the same issue at a Startup client.

BACK TO FORM
  Click "Back to Form" to analyse another candidate.


================================================================================
                              EXAMPLE
================================================================================

Candidate:  Sarah Tan, Project Manager, 5 years experience, 1 prior rejection
Mode:       Pre-Interview
Client:     Corporate

Scores:     Communication 5.0, Confidence 5.5, Technical 7.5,
            Cultural Fit 7.0, Internal 7.0

Notes:      "Seemed nervous during prep call. Hadn't researched the client."

Result:     HIGH RISK (~52/100)
Issues:     Low communication, low confidence, underprepared, rejection penalty
Action:     Run coaching sessions before submitting.


================================================================================
                              QUICK REFERENCE
================================================================================

RISK LEVELS:
  LOW 75-100 | MEDIUM 55-74 | HIGH 35-54 | CRITICAL 0-34

REJECTION PENALTY:
  -5 per rejection, maximum -20

KEYWORDS DETECTED:
  Communication  "unclear", "rambling", "struggled to articulate"
  Confidence     "nervous", "hesitant", "unsure", "lacked confidence"
  Technical      "wrong answer", "gaps in knowledge", "couldn't solve"
  Cultural Fit   "not a fit", "arrogant", "unprofessional"
  Preparation    "unprepared", "didn't research", "unaware of"
